# One-Day Meal Plan for Marathon Training Athlete

Here is a one-day meal plan for a vegetarian athlete training for a marathon with high energy demands:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3000 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (150g), Fat (67g), Carbs (450g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (6:30 AM):** Oatmeal (100g dry weight) with banana (120g), ground flaxseed (15g), and soy milk (240g).
    * Protein: 12g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 75g

* **Pre-Workout Snack (9:00 AM):** Whole wheat toast (60g) with peanut butter (25g) and honey (20g).
    * Protein: 10g, Fat: 10g, Carbs: 50g

* **Lunch (12:30 PM):** Chickpea and vegetable curry (200g chickpeas, 150g mixed vegetables) with brown rice (80g dry weight). Side of Greek yogurt (100g, non-fat).
    * Protein: 28g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 110g

* **Post-Workout Snack (3:30 PM):** Smoothie with Greek yogurt (150g non-fat), mixed berries (120g), oats (40g dry weight), and almond butter (20g).
    * Protein: 22g, Fat: 10g, Carbs: 65g

* **Dinner (7:00 PM):** Lentil and vegetable stir-fry (150g cooked lentils, 200g mixed vegetables) with quinoa (75g dry weight) and sesame oil (10g).
    * Protein: 35g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 95g

* **Evening Snack (9:30 PM):** Whole grain bread (50g) with hummus (40g) and carrot sticks (100g).
    * Protein: 8g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 35g

This meal plan emphasizes plant-based protein sources (legumes, tofu, Greek yogurt, nuts, and seeds) while providing sustained carbohydrate energy for marathon training and recovery support.
